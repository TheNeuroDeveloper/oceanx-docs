# Token Pages

Token pages provide a deeper view of individual assets.

They combine charts, trades, holders, wallet intelligence, risk signals and social discussion in one workspace.

## Chart

Token pages include realtime charting for supported assets.

Charts may show:

- Price
- Market cap
- Volume
- Lifecycle status
- Migration context

## Trades

View live and historical trade activity.

Trade feeds may include:

- Buy and sell activity
- Wallet identities
- Trade size
- Timestamps
- Wallet status

## Holders

Holder analytics help users understand token ownership.

Metrics may include:

- Holder count
- Top holder concentration
- Top 10 percentage
- Distribution changes

## Top Traders

Top Traders helps identify wallets that performed well on a specific token.

This is designed for research and context, not copy trading.

## First Buyers

First Buyers shows early participants in a token's lifecycle.

This can help users understand who entered early and whether notable wallets were involved.

## Dev Tokens

Dev Tokens shows other assets associated with the same creator or developer where available.

## Safety Analysis

OceanX displays risk and safety signals such as:

- Sniper activity
- Insider activity
- Developer holdings
- Top holder concentration
- Contract or wallet flags
- Low, medium or high risk labels

## Positions

Connected wallet positions may show:

- Current holdings
- Entry price
- Realized PnL
- Unrealized PnL
- Position history

## Chat

Token pages may include token-specific chat rooms for discussion and community activity.

## FAQ

### Does OceanX support copy trading?

Not yet.

### Are risk scores guarantees?

No. Risk labels and metrics are informational tools only.
