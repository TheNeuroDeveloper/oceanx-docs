# Portfolio

Portfolio provides a view of assets you hold, have held and have traded.

It helps users understand performance across positions and timeframes.

## Portfolio metrics

OceanX may display:

- Overall PnL
- Realized PnL
- Unrealized PnL
- PnL by timeframe
- Win rate
- Average hold time
- Best trade
- Worst trade
- Current holdings
- Historical positions

## Timeframes

Users may review performance across different periods, such as:

- 24 hours
- 7 days
- 30 days
- 90 days
- Lifetime

## Holdings

Holdings show assets currently held in your OceanX wallets.

Information may include:

- Asset
- Quantity
- Current price
- Current value
- Entry price
- Realized PnL
- Unrealized PnL
- Network

## Historical trades

Portfolio analytics include assets you have previously traded, not just assets currently held.

## CSV exports

CSV exports are planned.

OceanX does not currently provide tax reporting functionality.

## FAQ

### Does Portfolio track realized PnL?

Yes.

### Does Portfolio include assets I no longer hold?

Yes. Portfolio is designed to include assets you hold, have held and have traded.

### Does OceanX provide tax reports?

No. CSV exports are planned, but tax reporting is not currently provided.
