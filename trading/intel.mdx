# Intel

Intel is OceanX's wallet intelligence and monitoring product.

It helps users follow wallets, understand activity and discover market opportunities.

<Note>
Intel is not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Wallet tracking

Intel can be used to monitor wallets and activity such as:

- Buys
- Sells
- Transfers
- Holdings
- Realized PnL
- Unrealized PnL
- Position changes

## Watchlists

Wallet watchlists are planned.

Users will be able to follow wallets and organize activity into watchlists.

## Labels

Users may label wallets for easier tracking.

Examples include:

- Whale
- Smart money
- Insider
- Developer
- Friend
- Market maker
- KOL (Key Opinion Leader)

## Alerts

Intel is designed to support alerts based on custom criteria.

Examples include:

- Wallet buys over a threshold
- Wallet sells over a threshold
- Wallet enters a new token
- Developer launches a token
- Whale enters a position
- PnL crosses a threshold

## Notification channels

| Channel | Status | Description |
