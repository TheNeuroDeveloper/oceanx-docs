# Orders

OceanX orders are designed to reduce friction while giving traders more control over execution.

## Market orders

Market orders execute immediately using the OceanX routing engine.

Orders are:

- Placed directly through the OceanX UI
- Automatically signed using embedded wallet infrastructure
- Simulated
- Smart-routed
- Submitted for execution

## Limit orders

Limit orders are planned.

They will be placed directly through the OceanX interface and automatically signed by the user's wallet infrastructure where supported.

## Planned order types

OceanX plans to support additional order types, including:

- Stop Loss
- Take Profit
- Trailing Stop
- DCA

DCA details are still to be confirmed.

## FAQ

### Do I need to manually sign every order?

No. OceanX is designed around embedded wallet signing and one-click execution.

### Is DCA live?

DCA is planned. Details will be confirmed in future updates.
