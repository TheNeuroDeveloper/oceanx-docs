# OceanX Documentation

OceanX is an all in one trading, research and market intelligence platform built for digital asset traders.

The platform combines noncustodial wallets, fast execution, real time market discovery, portfolio analytics, wallet intelligence, social trading and AI powered research in one interface.

OceanX is designed for traders who want to move quickly without giving up ownership of their assets.

## Core principles

### Self custody

OceanX wallets are noncustodial. Users remain in control of their wallets and assets. Fast trading uses session based signing within limits you authorize. See [Wallets & Security](/getting-started/wallets-security).

### Speed

OceanX is designed for fast trading workflows, including one click execution, smart routing and optimized execution for newly migrated assets.

### Intelligence

OceanX combines real time data, wallet intelligence and AI powered research to help users make better trading decisions.

### Community

OceanX includes social profiles, chat, groups, voice rooms, badges, leaderboards and competitions.
