# XP

XP represents user progression inside OceanX.

It is earned through platform activity and contributes to rewards, tiers and leaderboards.

## How to earn XP

Users can earn XP through:

- Trading volume
- Daily quests
- Referrals

Additional XP sources may be added in the future.

## Lifetime XP

XP is currently lifetime-based.

This may change based on community feedback.

## Tiers

XP and points are connected to OceanX's tier system.
