# Competitions

OceanX will host competitions to reward active users and communities.

Competitions may include cash prizes, badges, partner rewards and more.

<Note>
Competitions are not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Competition types

| Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Trading volume | Rankings based on total volume on eligible products during the competition window |
| PnL | Rankings based on realized or total profit and loss |
| ROI | Rankings based on return on investment percentage |
| Referrals | Rankings based on new users referred during the competition |
| Points | Rankings based on points earned during the competition period |
| Scout discoveries | Rankings based on early discovery or watchlist performance |
| Community activity | Rankings based on social participation, groups or voice room engagement |

## Participation

Some competitions may use public leaderboards.

Others may require opt in participation.

## Competition pages

Live competitions will have dedicated pages with:

- Rules
- Start and end dates
- Leaderboards
- Rewards
- Eligibility details
