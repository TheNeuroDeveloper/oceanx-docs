# Seasons

OceanX plans to start with seasonal competitions and campaigns.

Seasons give users a structured way to compete, earn XP and unlock rewards.

## Seasonal rewards

Rewards may include:

- Cash prizes
- Badges
- Partner rewards
- XP boosts
- Cashback boosts
- Ecosystem incentives

## XP and seasons

XP is currently lifetime-based.

OceanX may review season and XP mechanics based on community feedback.
