# Seasons

OceanX plans to start with seasonal competitions and campaigns.

Seasons give users a structured way to compete, earn points and unlock rewards.

<Note>
Seasons are not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Seasonal rewards

Rewards may include:

- Cash prizes
- Badges
- Partner rewards
- Points boosts
- Cashback boosts (meme coin trading only)
- Ecosystem incentives

## Points and seasons

Points are currently lifetime based.

OceanX may review season and points mechanics based on community feedback.
