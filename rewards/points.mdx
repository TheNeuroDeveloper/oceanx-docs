# Points

Points represent user progression inside OceanX.

They are earned through platform activity and contribute to rewards, tiers and leaderboards.

## How to earn points

Users can earn points through:

- Trading volume on eligible products
- Daily quests
- Referrals

Additional point sources may be added in the future.

## Lifetime points

Points are currently lifetime based.

This may change based on community feedback.

## Tiers

Points determine your tier in OceanX's rewards system.

| Tier | Points Multiplier | Cashback |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Ripple | 1x | 0% |
| Wave | 2x | 5% |
| Current | 3x | 10% |
| Surge | 4x | 20% |
| Storm | 5x | 30% |
| Tsunami | 6x | 45% |
| Poseidon | 7x | 60% |

<Note>
Cashback percentages apply to meme coin trading fees only. Other products are not live yet and do not earn cashback at this time.
</Note>
