# Getting Started

OceanX is designed to get users from signup to trading quickly.

After creating an account, OceanX provisions an embedded wallet setup for you:

- One Solana wallet
- One EVM compatible wallet

You can then deposit assets, configure security settings and begin exploring the platform.

## Basic flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your email and verify your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Secure your login">
    Set your password or passkey and configure additional security options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet">
    Deposit crypto from another wallet or exchange. Include native tokens (SOL, ETH) for gas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore OceanX">
    Use Scout, Portfolio, Intel, Research and Social to discover and manage opportunities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Do I need KYC?

Most OceanX products do not require identity verification.

OceanX is primarily a DeFi platform. However, some partner products may require KYC, identity checks or jurisdiction screening. This may include xStocks, fiat services, tokenized securities or other regulated integrations.

## Supported assets

OceanX supports a growing selection of digital assets.

Support may expand over time as new products, networks and integrations are added.
