# Creating an Account

Creating an OceanX account takes less than a minute.

OceanX uses embedded wallet infrastructure, allowing users to get started without installing browser extensions or manually managing seed phrases.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up with your email">
    Visit OceanX and enter your email address to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    OceanX sends a verification code to your email. Enter the code to confirm ownership of the account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your password or passkey">
    Add a secure login method. Passkeys are recommended where supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trading platform created">
    Once verified, OceanX provisions your trading environment and embedded wallets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Wallet creation

When your account is created, OceanX initially provisions:

- One Solana wallet
- One EVM compatible wallet

These wallets are noncustodial and controlled by you.

## What you can access after signup

After creating an account, you can access:

- Scout
- Portfolio
- Wallets
- Research
- Intel
- Social
- Rewards
- Referrals

## FAQ

### Do I need a browser wallet?

No. OceanX uses embedded wallets, so you do not need to install a browser extension to start.

### Can I still use external wallets?

External wallets are possible, but you need to import your existing wallet seed phrase into OceanX. OceanX does not support browser extension wallet connections at launch. Onboarding is built around auto provisioned embedded wallets.

### Do I need KYC to create an account?

No. Most OceanX products do not require KYC. Some partner products may require verification.
