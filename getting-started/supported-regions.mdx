# Supported Regions

OceanX is designed to be accessible globally in most jurisdictions.

Access may be restricted where required by sanctions, laws, regulations, partner restrictions or platform policies.

## Restricted jurisdictions

OceanX may restrict access for users located in, resident in or otherwise subject to sanctions imposed by the United Nations, United States, European Union, United Kingdom or other applicable authorities.

Restricted jurisdictions may include, but are not limited to:

- Russia
- Belarus
- Iran
- North Korea
- Syria
- Cuba
- Crimea
- Donetsk People's Republic
- Luhansk People's Republic
- Any jurisdiction subject to comprehensive sanctions, embargoes or trade restrictions

## Product-level restrictions

Certain products may have additional geographic restrictions.

This may include:

- xStocks
- Fiat services
- Tokenized securities
- Yield products
- Prediction markets
- Third-party partner integrations

Restrictions may be based on issuer requirements, provider obligations or local laws.

## Changes to availability

OceanX may update supported and restricted jurisdictions at any time.

Users are responsible for ensuring their use of OceanX is permitted under applicable laws in their jurisdiction.

## FAQ

### Is OceanX global?

OceanX is available in most jurisdictions, except restricted or sanctioned regions.

### Can partner products have different rules?

Yes. Some products may apply additional checks or restrictions.
