# Funding Your Wallet

Before trading, you need to fund your OceanX wallet.

OceanX initially provisions one Solana wallet and one EVM compatible wallet for each user.

## Deposit assets

To fund your wallet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your wallet or portfolio">
    Navigate to the wallet, portfolio or funding area inside OceanX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a network">
    Select Solana or a supported EVM compatible network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your address">
    Copy the deposit address shown in OceanX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send assets">
    Send assets from another wallet, exchange or custodian.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported networks

OceanX currently supports Solana and major EVM compatible chains.

This may include:

- Ethereum
- Base
- Arbitrum
- Optimism
- Polygon
- BNB Chain
- Avalanche

Supported networks may change as OceanX expands.

## Withdrawals

Users can withdraw assets from their OceanX wallets to external addresses.

OceanX does not charge withdrawal fees.

Standard network gas fees still apply and are paid from your wallet balance on each chain.

## Gas for embedded wallets

Your embedded wallet holds the native tokens needed to pay network gas:

- **Solana:** SOL pays transaction fees
- **EVM chains:** ETH or the chain's native token (e.g. MATIC on Polygon) pays transaction fees

When you deposit funds, include enough of the native token to cover expected trading, transfer and withdrawal fees. OceanX does not subsidize gas.

Transactions are signed by your wallet. OceanX does not custody funds.

## Fiat onramp

Fiat onramp support is planned.

Availability, payment methods and jurisdiction support will depend on integrated providers.

## FAQ

### Can I deposit from an exchange?

Yes, provided the exchange supports the asset and network you are depositing to.

### Does OceanX charge deposit fees?

OceanX generally does not charge a platform fee for deposits. Network fees may apply.

### Does OceanX charge withdrawal fees?

No. OceanX does not charge withdrawal fees. You still pay standard network gas fees from your wallet balance.

### Does OceanX support fiat purchases?

Fiat onramp functionality is planned.

### Where does gas come from for embedded wallets?

From your wallet balance on each chain. Deposit the chain's native token (SOL, ETH, etc.) alongside your trading assets to cover transaction fees.
