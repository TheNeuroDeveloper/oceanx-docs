# Funding Your Wallet

Before trading, you need to fund your OceanX wallet.

OceanX initially provisions one Solana wallet and one EVM-compatible wallet for each user.

## Deposit assets

To fund your wallet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your wallet or portfolio">
    Navigate to the wallet, portfolio or funding area inside OceanX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a network">
    Select Solana or a supported EVM-compatible network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your address">
    Copy the deposit address shown in OceanX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send assets">
    Send assets from another wallet, exchange or custodian.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported networks

OceanX currently supports Solana and major EVM-compatible chains.

This may include:

- Ethereum
- Base
- Arbitrum
- Optimism
- Polygon
- BNB Chain
- Avalanche

Supported networks may change as OceanX expands.

## Withdrawals

Users can withdraw assets from their OceanX wallets to external wallets.

OceanX does not custody funds. Transactions are signed by the user's wallet.

## Fiat on-ramp

Fiat on-ramp support is planned.

Availability, payment methods and jurisdiction support will depend on integrated providers.

## FAQ

### Can I deposit from an exchange?

Yes, provided the exchange supports the asset and network you are depositing to.

### Does OceanX charge deposit fees?

OceanX generally does not charge a platform fee for deposits. Network fees may apply.

### Does OceanX support fiat purchases?

Fiat on-ramp functionality is planned.
