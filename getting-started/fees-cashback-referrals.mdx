# Fees, Cashback & Referrals

OceanX uses a simple fee and rewards model designed to reward active users.

## Trading fees

OceanX generally charges a flat 1% fee.

Fees may vary by product or integration.

Examples include:

- Meme coin trading
- Perpetuals
- xStocks
- Yield products
- Partner services

Applicable fees are shown inside the OceanX interface where relevant.

## Cashback

Users can reduce effective fees through cashback tiers.

Cashback currently applies primarily to meme coin trading and may differ for other services.

| Tier | Points Multiplier | Cashback |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Ripple | 1x | 0% |
| Wave | 2x | 5% |
| Current | 3x | 10% |
| Surge | 4x | 20% |
| Storm | 5x | 30% |
| Tsunami | 6x | 45% |

## Referrals

OceanX includes a three-level referral network.

| Network Level | Reward |
| --- | ---: |
| Level 1 | 30% |
| Level 2 | 3% |
| Level 3 | 2% |

Level 1 refers to direct referrals.

Level 2 refers to users invited by your referrals.

Level 3 refers to third-degree referral activity.

## Points and XP

Users can earn points and XP through:

- Trading volume
- Daily quests
- Referrals

Additional ways to earn may be added in the future.

## FAQ

### Are fees always 1%?

OceanX typically charges a 1% flat fee, but fees may vary by service.

### Does cashback apply to every product?

Not necessarily. Cashback currently applies primarily to meme coin trading. Other products may use separate fee or reward structures.

### Can referral rates change?

Yes. OceanX may update referral rewards as the platform evolves.
