# Workspaces

Workspaces help users organize research.

Users can create custom workspace names for different strategies, markets or workflows.

## Examples

Common workspace examples include:

- Solana Alpha
- Memecoin Research
- Macro Markets
- Perpetual Strategies
- Whale Tracking
- Long-Term Ideas

## Saved chats

OceanX saves research chats so users can return to previous analysis.

## Organizing research

Users can use workspaces to separate:

- Token due diligence
- Wallet analysis
- KOL research
- News monitoring
- Portfolio reviews
- Trading strategies

## FAQ

### Can I name my own workspaces?

Yes.

### Are chats saved?

Yes. Research chats can be saved and revisited.
