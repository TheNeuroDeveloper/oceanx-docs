# Research Overview

OceanX Research combines proprietary AI systems with market data, wallet intelligence and news.

It is designed to help users analyze opportunities, understand context and move faster from idea to decision.

## What Research can help with

Research may support:

- Token analysis
- Wallet analysis
- Market summaries
- News interpretation
- KOL (Key Opinion Leader) research
- Whale monitoring
- Strategy review
- Macro and cross market context

## AI access

OceanX uses proprietary AI systems.

Users may need credits to access certain AI features.

## Credits

Research credits may be acquired within OceanX.

Credits may be connected to OceanX token holdings, direct purchases or promotional campaigns.

Credits expire after twelve months.

## Portfolio context

OceanX AI can access user portfolio context where permitted, helping provide more relevant analysis.

## FAQ

### Does OceanX use image generation?

No.

### Can AI see my portfolio?

Yes, where the user has permissioned or enabled relevant context.
