# AI Assistant

The OceanX AI Assistant helps users research markets and interpret information.

## Example uses

Users can ask the assistant to:

- Summarize a token
- Analyze a wallet
- Compare assets
- Review recent market activity
- Explain risk indicators
- Summarize news
- Evaluate a trading idea
- Identify unusual wallet behavior

## Proprietary AI systems

OceanX leverages proprietary AI systems.

The exact model stack is not publicly documented.

## Credits

Some AI activity may require credits.

Credits expire after twelve months.

## No financial advice

AI outputs are informational only.

Users should make their own decisions and understand the risks of trading.
