# Voice

Voice rooms allow users to talk in real time inside OceanX.

<Note>
Voice is not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Use cases

Voice can be used for:

- Trading sessions
- Market commentary
- Community events
- Competitions
- Research discussions
- Token rooms

## Access

Voice rooms may include both:

- Open rooms
- Invite only rooms

## FAQ

### Are voice rooms public?

Some may be open, while others may be invite only.

### Can groups use voice?

Yes, voice can support group and community use cases.
