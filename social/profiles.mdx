# Profiles

Each OceanX account can have a user profile.

Profiles help users build identity and reputation inside the platform.

## Profile fields

Profiles may include:

- Username
- Avatar
- Bio
- XP
- Tier
- Badges
- Social links

## Social links

Users can connect:

- X
- Discord
- Telegram

## Verification

Verification badges are invite-only.

OceanX may verify selected users, partners, contributors or notable community members.

## FAQ

### Can anyone get verified?

No. Verification is invite-only.

### Are usernames anonymous?

No. OceanX uses custom handles that are unique within OceanX.
