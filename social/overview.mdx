# Social Overview

OceanX includes a social layer built directly into the trading platform.

Users can discuss markets, join token rooms, build groups and follow other traders.

## Core features

Social may include:

- Profiles
- Custom handles
- Token rooms
- Chat
- Voice rooms
- Groups
- Follows
- Badges
- Social links

## Usernames

Usernames are unique within OceanX.

OceanX does not use anonymous usernames by default.

## Social connections

Users can connect:

- X
- Discord
- Telegram

ENS support is not currently planned.

## FAQ

### Are usernames unique?

Yes, usernames are unique within OceanX.

### Can I connect my social accounts?

Yes. X, Discord and Telegram connections are supported or planned.
