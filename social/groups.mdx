# Groups

Groups allow users to organize communities inside OceanX.

## Group types

Groups may be used for:

- Trading teams
- Research groups
- Alpha communities
- Friends
- DAO communities
- Competition teams

## Direct messages

Direct messages are planned.

## Invite and open access

Groups may support both open and invite-based access depending on configuration.

## FAQ

### Are DMs planned?

Yes.

### Can groups be invite-only?

Yes, invite-based group access is planned.
