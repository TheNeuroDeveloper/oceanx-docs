Track any Solana wallet and get real-time trade alerts — straight from Telegram, tied to your OceanX account.

<Note>
  The Telegram bot is **free** while in beta.
</Note>

## Connect Telegram to OceanX

Before the bot can track anything for you, link your Telegram to your OceanX account. You only need to do this once.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to OceanX">
    Sign in at [oceanx.trade](https://www.oceanx.trade).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Connected accounts">
    Go to **Settings → Profile → Connected accounts**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Telegram">
    Next to **Telegram**, click **Log in with Telegram** and approve the popup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

There's no API key to copy anywhere. Your Telegram is now linked and the bot can act on your behalf.

## Commands

Open [@OceanXTradeBot](https://t.me/OceanXTradeBot) in Telegram:

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/track <wallet>` | Start tracking a Solana wallet. |
| `/wallets` | List every wallet you're tracking. |
| `/untrack <wallet>` | Stop tracking a wallet. |
| `/trades` | Show recent trades across your tracked wallets. |
| _(automatic)_ | Live alerts — the bot messages you the moment a tracked wallet trades. |

## Same data as the OceanX app

Your tracked wallets are shared with the in-app [Tracker](/trading/intel):

- Add a wallet in the bot → it appears in the web app.
- Add a wallet in the app → it appears with `/wallets` in Telegram.
- Alerts and trade history come from the same live feed.

<Note>
  Tracking begins the moment you add a wallet — there's no historical backfill, so a freshly tracked wallet stays quiet until it next trades.
</Note>

## Disconnect

To stop everything, go to **Settings → Profile → Connected accounts** and disconnect Telegram. The bot immediately loses access and alerts stop.

Disconnecting does not delete your OceanX account.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Connect your account" reply** — you haven't linked yet (or you disconnected). Re-link at **Settings → Profile → Connected accounts**.
- **Nothing after `/track`** — tracking is live-only; the wallet simply hasn't traded since you added it.
