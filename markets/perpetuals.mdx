# Perpetuals

OceanX Perpetuals provide leveraged trading directly inside OceanX.

Perpetual trading is powered by Hyperliquid.

## Trading from OceanX

Users can trade directly from their OceanX wallet within OceanX.

No separate interface is required.

## Features

OceanX Perpetuals may include:

- Market selection
- Order entry
- Leverage controls
- Order book
- Charting
- Position management
- Live PnL
- Position history

## Fees

Hyperliquid fees apply.

Applicable fees are displayed inside the OceanX interface.

## Risk

Perpetual futures involve leverage and may result in significant losses.

Users should understand liquidation risk before trading.
