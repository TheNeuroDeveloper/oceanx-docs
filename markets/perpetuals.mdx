# Perpetuals

OceanX Perpetuals provide leveraged trading directly inside OceanX.

Perpetual trading is powered by Hyperliquid.

<Note>
Perpetuals are not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Trading from OceanX

Users can trade directly from their OceanX wallet within OceanX.

No separate interface is required.

## Order types

Perpetuals support market and limit orders through Hyperliquid.

This is different from spot meme coin trading on OceanX, where limit orders are not yet available. See [Orders](/trading/orders) for the current spot order type roadmap.

## Features

OceanX Perpetuals may include:

- Market and limit order entry
- Market selection
- Leverage controls
- Order book
- Charting
- Position management
- Live PnL
- Position history

## Fees

Hyperliquid fees apply.

Applicable fees are displayed inside the OceanX interface.

## Risk

Perpetual futures involve leverage and may result in significant losses.

Users should understand liquidation risk before trading.
