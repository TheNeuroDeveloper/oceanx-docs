# Yield

Yield products are under development.

OceanX plans to support lending and structured products through the platform.

<Note>
Yield is not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Planned yield products

Yield may include:

- **Lending vaults** — Deposit assets to earn yield from lending markets. TVL (Total Value Locked), APY (Annual Percentage Yield) and supported assets will be shown on each vault page.
- **Structured products** — Curated yield strategies with defined risk and return profiles.
- **Position tracking** — Monitor open yield positions, accrued rewards and withdrawal status in one view.

## Trading interface features

When Yield launches, the OceanX interface will include:

- Vault discovery and comparison
- Deposit and withdrawal flows
- Live APY and TVL display
- Position history and performance tracking

## Availability

Yield details, supported assets and APY methodology will be shared closer to launch.

## Risk

Yield products may involve smart contract, market, liquidity and counterparty risks.

Users should review each product's risk disclosures before depositing. Counterparty risk applies where yield depends on a third party protocol, custodian or market maker. OceanX does not guarantee returns on yield products.
