# Predict

Predict is OceanX's planned prediction market product.

It is intended to let users explore and trade event based markets from within OceanX.

<Note>
Predict is not live yet. Release date TBC.
</Note>

## Planned features

Predict may include:

- Market categories
- Event markets
- Price history
- Market detail pages
- Positions
- Trade history

## Availability

Predict is under development.

Additional details will be shared closer to launch.
