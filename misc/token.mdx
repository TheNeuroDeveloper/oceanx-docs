# OceanX Token

<Warning>
The OceanX token has not launched. There is no official contract address.
</Warning>

Any token claiming to represent OceanX should be considered unofficial unless announced through official OceanX channels.

## Planned utility

The OceanX token is planned to support ecosystem utility such as:

- Platform discounts
- Governance
- Staking
- Fee share from the platform

Details may change before launch.

## Safety notice

Do not trust unofficial contract addresses, DMs, fake websites or impersonators.

Always verify token information through official OceanX channels.
