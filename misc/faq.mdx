# FAQ

## General

### What is OceanX?

OceanX is an all-in-one trading, research and market intelligence platform for digital asset traders.

### Do I need a browser wallet?

No. OceanX uses embedded wallet infrastructure.

### Does OceanX require KYC?

Most OceanX products do not require KYC. Some partner products may require verification.

### Which assets does OceanX support?

OceanX supports a growing selection of digital assets.

### Which networks does OceanX support?

OceanX supports Solana and major EVM-compatible networks.

## Trading

### What are OceanX fees?

OceanX generally charges a flat 1% fee, though fees may vary by service.

### Does OceanX support one-click trading?

Yes. OceanX supports one-click execution using embedded wallet signing.

### Does OceanX have trading limits?

OceanX currently has no platform-level trading limits. External providers may apply their own limits.

## Rewards

### How do referrals work?

OceanX has a three-level referral model: 30% for Level 1, 3% for Level 2 and 2% for Level 3.

### How do I earn XP?

XP can be earned through trading volume, daily quests and referrals.

## Token

### Has the OceanX token launched?

No. The OceanX token has not launched and there is no official contract address.

## Research

### Does OceanX Research use AI?

Yes. OceanX uses proprietary AI systems.

### Do research credits expire?

Yes. Research credits expire after twelve months.

## Social

### Are usernames unique?

Yes. Usernames are unique within OceanX.

### Are DMs planned?

Yes. Direct messages and groups are planned.
